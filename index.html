About Us

Your path to mental wellness

Nubi Wellness Center was born from a lack of quality services in the area of mental health which has been neglected for so long. That is why we strive to improve the quality of care available to those in need of mental health care

Our team of experts are ready to provide competent, effective, reliable and timely mental health care to nigerians who are seeking treatment. we seek to make our patients experience with us second to none.

The ambience at the facility is uplifting and most importantly, our staff are courteous and professional. From the well trained security team to the experenced and friendly nurses, you are guaranteed empathetic support from the medical staff.

Safety and comfort whilst having healthy and tasty meals from our in-house chef is guaranteed.
